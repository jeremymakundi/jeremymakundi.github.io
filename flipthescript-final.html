You Know There's A Gap. Let's Close It.

Most people feel the distance between where they are and what they're capable of. They just haven't named what's sitting in it. I help people name it, understand it, and remove it.

The Problem I Solve

People use crutches to avoid addressing what's underneath. Confidence gaps. Perceived stress. Inability to communicate. Social anxiety. The crutch is different for everyone — but the most socially accepted, most normalized, most unexamined one is alcohol.

I spent 28 years using it to fill the gap between where I was and what I could be. When I removed it, I understood the gap for the first time. That understanding became FlipTheScript™ and The Gap Framework™.

Alcohol should not be an option. Stop outsourcing your potential.
